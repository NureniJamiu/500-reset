# The 500 Reset

## Overview
**The 500 Reset** is a climate sustainability and AI-assisted recycling initiative focused on transforming plastic waste into economic value for underserved communities. The name stems from the harsh reality that plastics can take up to 500 years to decompose. Our mission is to "reset" that future by creating a smarter, more rewarding recycling ecosystem.

## Goals
- Provide actionable **climate & sustainability solutions**.
- Leverage **AI and automation** for social good.
- Drive **economic empowerment** through recycling.

## Proposed Solution
An AI-assisted recycling platform that connects individuals and communities directly to recycling opportunities, rewarding proper plastic waste disposal. The platform focuses on:
- Recycling plastic waste.
- Monetizing recycling participation.
- Creating easier access between recyclers and communities.

## Core Idea
Users collect and dispose of recyclable plastics through the platform and receive points or rewards based on their recycling activity.

## Feature Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP (Starting Simple)
- **User Accounts & Authentication:** Basic signup and login.
- **QR-Based Recycling Tracking:** Scan codes to track disposals.
- **Reward Points/Cash System:** Wallet to view earned points and cash value.
- **Collection Pickup Requests:** Request a pickup for accumulated plastics.

### Phase 2: Community & Scale (Future)
- **Student Recycling Leaderboard:** Gamify recycling in schools and universities.
- **Market Recycling Hubs:** Dedicated drop-off points with aggregated stats.
- **Recycling Company Integrations:** Direct connection for bulk recycling sales.

## Technology Stack
- **Framework:** [React Native](https://reactnative.dev/) (via [Expo](https://expo.dev/))
- **Styling:** [Gluestack UI](https://gluestack.io/)

### Why Expo?
Expo is the ideal choice for this project:
1. **Android Studio Emulator:** You can easily run the app in your emulator by starting the dev server (`npx expo start`) and pressing `a`.
2. **Mobile Device Preview:** You can instantly preview the app on your physical iOS or Android device by downloading the **Expo Go** app and scanning the QR code shown in your terminal. This makes testing fast and frictionless.
3. **Gluestack UI Support:** Gluestack UI integrates beautifully with Expo, and they provide quick-start configurations specifically tailored for it.
